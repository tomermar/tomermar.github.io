I am a Senior Lecturer (equivalent to Assistant Professor) at the School of Mechanical Engineering at Tel Aviv University. Prior to my current position I was a postdoctoral research associate at
the Center for Theoretical Biological Physics, Rice Univeristy,
working with Prof. Fred C. MacKintosh.
and a Blavatnik postdoctoral fellow in the group of Prof. Michael E. Cates
at the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics,
University of Cambridge.
My PhD was done at Tel Aviv University
under the supervision of Prof. David Andelman.


I am opening a new group in The School of Mechanical Engineering at Tel Aviv University in the fall of 2022!
